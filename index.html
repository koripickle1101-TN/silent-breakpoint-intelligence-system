Core Thesis

Billing is where failure arrives.

It is not always where failure begins. The real breakpoints often start earlier in intake documentation authorization handoffs and invisible workload.

Healthcare Systems That Break Silently

The most expensive healthcare problems rarely start in billing.

They start upstream in workflow. They appear later as claim denials delayed reimbursement staff rework patient access delays burnout and leadership firefighting.

Sample Use Case

Prior Authorization Delay

A prior authorization request remains unresolved because no one owns the aging queue after submission. The problem becomes visible when care is delayed and reimbursement risk increases. The fix is an authorization aging tracker with assigned ownership escalation rules and service date prioritization.
